# Analyst Practice Center Guide

Welcome to the Analyst Practice Center, your integrated environment for mastering financial analysis, credit risk assessment, and quantitative modeling. This guide provides an overview of the available tools and how to use them effectively.

## 1. Dashboard (Mission Control)
Your central hub for tracking progress.
- **Active Projects:** View ongoing simulations and assignments.
- **Quick Actions:** Shortcuts to the most frequently used tools.
- **Alerts:** Notifications about new content or potential credit risks in your simulated portfolio.

## 2. Learning & Simulations
### Learning Paths
Structured curriculums designed to take you from beginner to advanced levels in specific domains (e.g., "Credit Analyst Foundation", "CFA Level 1 Prep").

### Simulation Lab
Interactive "Choose Your Own Adventure" style scenarios where you play the role of an analyst. You will be presented with data, asked to make decisions, and receive feedback on your choices.
*   **Tip:** Treat these as real-world assignments. Take notes and justify your decisions.

### Coding Lab
A practice environment for financial programming (Python/JavaScript).
- **Snippet Library:** Click on items in the sidebar to load pre-written code snippets for common tasks (e.g., WACC calculation, Black-Scholes).
- **Execution:** Run code to see immediate output (simulated for Python).

## 3. Analyst Toolkit
### Valuation Console
*   **DCF Calculator:** Estimate Enterprise Value using a simplified Discounted Cash Flow model.
*   **EV Bridge:** Calculate Enterprise Value from Equity Value and debt items.
*   **Waterfall Tool:** Model the distribution of exit proceeds among different classes of creditors and equity holders.
    *   *Pro Tip:* You can link the output of the DCF or EV calculator directly to the Waterfall tool.

### Legal Library & Covenant Builder
A comprehensive database of standard legal clauses found in credit agreements.
- **Browse:** Select a category (e.g., Negative Covenants, Intercreditor Agreements) to see standard clauses.
- **Build:** Click "Insert" to add clauses to your working document.
- **Learn:** Each clause includes "Negotiation Points" to help you understand what to look for during deal structuring.

### Deal Screener
Filter a database of mock LBO deals based on criteria like Sector, Leverage, and Margins. Use this to practice identifying attractive investment targets.

### Credit Rating Console
A tool to estimate a synthetic credit rating for a company.
- Input financial ratios (e.g., Debt/EBITDA, Interest Coverage).
- Answer qualitative business risk questions.
- The model calculates a score and assigns a rating (AAA to D) based on a weighted methodology.

## 4. AI Command Center
### Prompt Library
A curated collection of prompts designed for financial analysts using Large Language Models (LLMs).
- **Categories:** Filter by ESG, Forensic Accounting, Macro analysis, etc.
- **Usage:** Copy prompts to use with your preferred AI assistant to accelerate your workflow.

## 5. Glossary
A searchable database of financial terms.
- **Flashcards:** Switch to Flashcard mode to test your knowledge.
