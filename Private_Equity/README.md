# Private Equity

## Overview
Private Equity (PE) involves investing in private companies or taking public companies private. This pillar covers the core mechanics of how PE firms operate, how they value targets, and how they structure deals to maximize returns.

## Key Concepts
1.  **Leveraged Buyout (LBO)**: The acquisition of another company using a significant amount of borrowed money to meet the cost of acquisition.
2.  **Internal Rate of Return (IRR)**: The metric used to judge the profitability of an investment.
3.  **Multiple on Invested Capital (MOIC)**: A measure of how much money you made on your investment (e.g., 2.0x means you doubled your money).

## Tools
*   **LBO Modeler**: A quick calculator to sensitivity test LBO returns.
