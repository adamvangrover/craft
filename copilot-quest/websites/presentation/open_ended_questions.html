Open-Ended Questions

This is your chance to show off your critical thinking and problem-solving skills. There are no right or wrong answers, but the best answers will be well-reasoned and well-supported.

Question 1

Describe a time when you had to work with a difficult team member. How did you handle the situation?

Scoring Rubric:

  • 1 Point: Briefly describes the situation.
  • 2 Points: Provides a detailed explanation of the situation and the steps taken to resolve it.
  • 3 Points: Demonstrates strong problem-solving and communication skills.

Discussion Points:

  • What made the team member difficult to work with?
  • What steps did you take to resolve the situation?
  • What was the outcome of the situation?
  • What did you learn from the experience?

Question 2

What are the biggest challenges facing our industry today? How can we overcome them?

Scoring Rubric:

  • 1 Point: Identifies one or two challenges.
  • 2 Points: Provides a comprehensive overview of the challenges and offers some potential solutions.
  • 3 Points: Demonstrates a deep understanding of the industry and offers innovative solutions.

Discussion Points:

  • What are the biggest challenges facing our industry?
  • What are the root causes of these challenges?
  • What are some potential solutions to these challenges?
  • What are the pros and cons of each solution?

Discussion

Use this time to discuss the open-ended questions with your team. Brainstorm ideas, share your perspectives, and come up with a comprehensive answer. Remember to listen to each other and build on each other's ideas.
