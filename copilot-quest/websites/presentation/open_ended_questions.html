Open-Ended Questions

Question 1

Describe a time when you had to work with a difficult team member. How did you handle the situation?

Scoring Rubric:

  • 1 Point: Briefly describes the situation.
  • 2 Points: Provides a detailed explanation of the situation and the steps taken to resolve it.
  • 3 Points: Demonstrates strong problem-solving and communication skills.

Question 2

What are the biggest challenges facing our industry today? How can we overcome them?

Scoring Rubric:

  • 1 Point: Identifies one or two challenges.
  • 2 Points: Provides a comprehensive overview of the challenges and offers some potential solutions.
  • 3 Points: Demonstrates a deep understanding of the industry and offers innovative solutions.

Discussion

Use this time to discuss the open-ended questions with your team. Brainstorm ideas, share your perspectives, and come up with a comprehensive answer.
