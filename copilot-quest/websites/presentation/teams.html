Meet the Teams

Welcome, adventurers! You have been sorted into four distinct teams, each with its own unique strengths and abilities. Get to know your fellow team members and prepare for the challenges ahead!

Team Logic

Analytical, strategic, and data-driven. They excel at solving complex problems. They are the masterminds of the group, always thinking one step ahead.

Fun Fact: Team Logic's favorite board game is chess.

Team Harmony

Collaborative, empathetic, and communicative. They are the glue that holds the group together. They are the diplomats of the group, always seeking consensus and understanding.

Fun Fact: Team Harmony's favorite activity is a group hug.

Team Action

Energetic, decisive, and results-oriented. They are always ready to take on a new challenge. They are the warriors of the group, always charging into battle.

Fun Fact: Team Action's favorite movie is "Die Hard".

Team Imagination

Creative, innovative, and visionary. They are the masters of out-of-the-box thinking. They are the artists of the group, always coming up with new and exciting ideas.

Fun Fact: Team Imagination's favorite book is "The Lord of the Rings".
