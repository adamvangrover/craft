Aerospace & Defense

The Aerospace & Defense (A&D) sector comprises companies that research, design, manufacture, and support aircraft, spacecraft, defense systems, and related components and services. Key subsectors include commercial aerospace and defense.

Tip: Focus on backlog quality, program execution, and government budget trends for defense; for commercial, watch air traffic demand and airline profitability.

Automotive

The automotive industry encompasses the design, development, manufacturing, marketing, and selling of motor vehicles. Highly cyclical and undergoing a major transition towards electrification and autonomous driving.

Rule of Thumb: Unit sales, inventory levels, and regional market share are critical metrics. High fixed costs mean profitability is sensitive to volume.

Consumer Products

Covers Consumer Staples (less cyclical food, beverages) and Consumer Discretionary (more cyclical apparel, luxury goods). Key drivers: brand strength, distribution, e-commerce, input costs.

Historical Analysis: Track shifts in consumer preferences (e.g., health & wellness, sustainability) and the impact of private labels.

Energy Oil & Gas

Covers exploration & production (upstream), transportation (midstream), and refining & marketing (downstream). Sensitive to commodity prices, geopolitics, and energy transition.

Guided Learning: Analyze oil price scenario impacts on E&P vs. midstream operators.

Financial Institutions

Includes banks, insurance, asset managers. Highly regulated, sensitive to interest rates, credit cycles. Key bank metrics: NIM, NPLs, CET1.

Case Study: Compare balance sheet and risks of a commercial bank vs. P&C insurer.

Healthcare

Pharmaceuticals, biotech, medical devices, providers, insurers. Driven by R&D, drug pipelines, demographics, reimbursement. Patent cliffs are a major risk.

Technology

Rapid innovation, short product lifecycles. Software (SaaS), hardware, semiconductors, IT services, internet, cloud, AI, cybersecurity. High R&D, growth focus.

Tips & Tricks: For SaaS, focus on ARR, CAC, LTV, and churn rate.

Utilities

Electricity, gas, water. Often regulated, stable returns (ROE on rate base). Significant capex for infrastructure and renewables.
