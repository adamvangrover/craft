Managing Critical Risks

AI Hallucinations

Risk: AI invents facts, figures, or sources.
Mitigation: Use Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to force AI to cite trusted internal documents. Always have a human-in-the-loop to validate critical outputs.

Algorithmic Bias

Risk: AI perpetuates historical biases from training data, leading to unfair decisions.
Mitigation: Conduct regular audits for bias. Use diverse and representative data for training and ensure model decisions are explainable.

Data Leakage

Risk: Employees paste sensitive information into public AI tools.
Mitigation: Enforce a strict policy against using public tools for proprietary data. Use only enterprise-grade, secure AI platforms.
