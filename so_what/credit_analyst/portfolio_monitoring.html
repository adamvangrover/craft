Portfolio Monitoring

The Importance of Portfolio Monitoring

Portfolio monitoring is the process of regularly reviewing the performance of the loans in your portfolio. It's a critical part of credit risk management, as it allows you to identify potential problems early and take action to mitigate them.

Key Activities in Portfolio Monitoring

  • Reviewing financial statements: This will help you to assess the borrower's financial health and identify any red flags.
  • Monitoring covenant compliance: This will help you to ensure that the borrower is meeting their obligations under the loan agreement.
  • Staying up-to-date on industry trends: This will help you to identify any potential risks to the borrower's business.
  • Communicating with the borrower: This will help you to get a better understanding of the borrower's business and any challenges they may be facing.
