The "So What?" Hub

Introduction

This page is dedicated to the most important question in financial analysis: "So what?". It's not enough to identify what numbers are moving and why. The real value comes from understanding the impact and why it matters. This hub provides resources to help you develop the skills to form a compelling narrative, think critically, and deliver persuasive analysis.
