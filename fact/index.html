Welcome to the FACT

Your central hub for developing core skills in financial analysis and credit assessment. Whether you are a new analyst or a seasoned professional, this toolkit provides the resources you need to excel.

Core Competencies

Financial Reporting and Analysis (FRA)

Master the art of interpreting financial statements and conducting rigorous financial analysis.

Probability of Default (PD)

Learn the methodologies to assess credit risk and determine the likelihood of default.

Regulatory Risk (RR)

Understand the regulatory frameworks that govern the financial industry and ensure compliance.
