Quick Reference Guide to Regulatory Ratings

This guide provides a high-level summary of the key characteristics of each regulatory rating. For more detailed information, please refer to the full rating profiles.

Rating Key Characteristics Common Triggers
Pass - Strong primary source of repayment
- Consistent financial performance

Special Mention - Potential weakness in primary repayment source

Substandard Accrual - Well-defined weakness that jeopardizes repayment
- Reliance on secondary repayment sources
- High leverage, covenant violations
- Borrower is still current on interest payments		 - Sustained operating losses
- Inability to service debt from cash flow

Substandard Non-Accrual - All the characteristics of Substandard Accrual
- Borrower is 90+ days past due on interest or principal		 - Missed payments
- Determination that interest is not collectible
Doubtful - All the characteristics of Substandard
- Collection in full is highly questionable and improbable
- A pending event could improve recovery prospects		 - Failed turnaround plan
- Deepening financial distress
- Reliance on a highly uncertain event for recovery
