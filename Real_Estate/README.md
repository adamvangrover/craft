# Real Estate Finance Module

This module explores the unique characteristics of Real Estate as an asset class, from property valuation and development to REITs and mortgage-backed securities.

## Core Components

1.  **Deep Dives**: In-depth articles.
    *   REIT Analysis: Understanding Real Estate Investment Trusts.
    *   Property Valuation: Cap Rates and NOI.
2.  **Tools**: Interactive calculators.
    *   Mortgage Calculator: Amortization schedules and payment analysis.

## Learning Objectives

*   Calculate Net Operating Income (NOI) and Capitalization Rates.
*   Understand the structure and metrics of REITs (FFO, AFFO).
*   Model commercial and residential mortgage amortization.
