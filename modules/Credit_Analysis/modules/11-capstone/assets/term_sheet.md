# DRAFT TERM SHEET
**Strictly Private and Confidential**

**Borrower:** Apex Logistics, Inc. ("Borrower")
**Lenders:** BigBank NA, and a syndicate of lenders arranged by BigBank.
**Facility:** $150,000,000 Senior Secured Credit Facilities comprising:
*   $50,000,000 Revolving Credit Facility ("Revolver")
*   $100,000,000 Term Loan B ("TLB")

**Purpose:** Refinance existing indebtedness, fund capital expenditures for fleet expansion, and general corporate purposes.

**Interest Rate:**
*   Revolver: SOFR + 3.50%
*   TLB: SOFR + 4.25% (0.50% Floor)

**Tenor:**
*   Revolver: 5 Years
*   TLB: 7 Years

**Amortization:**
*   TLB: 1.00% per annum, payable quarterly.

**Financial Covenants:**

1.  **Maximum Total Net Leverage Ratio:**
    *   Closing Date through Year 2: **8.00x**
    *   Year 3 onwards: **7.50x**
    *   *Note: Total Net Debt / Consolidated Adjusted EBITDA*

2.  **Minimum Fixed Charge Coverage Ratio:**
    *   **0.90x**
    *   *Note: (EBITDA - Capex) / (Interest + Scheduled Amortization)*

**Key Definitions:**

*   **Consolidated Adjusted EBITDA:** Net Income plus Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, Amortization, plus:
    *   Non-cash stock based compensation.
    *   Any extraordinary, unusual, or non-recurring expenses, losses, or charges.
    *   **Projected cost savings** and synergies from the "ApexRoute" software implementation (up to 25% of EBITDA).
    *   **Management Fees** paid to sponsors.

*   **Excess Cash Flow Sweep:**
    *   50% of ECF if Leverage > 4.0x
    *   0% of ECF if Leverage <= 4.0x

**Negative Covenants (Baskets):**

*   **Restricted Payments (Dividends):**
    *   Unlimited dividends permitted if Pro Forma Leverage Ratio is less than **6.00x**.
    *   Builder Basket: 100% of Net Income.

*   **Investments:**
    *   General basket: $50,000,000.
    *   Unlimited investments in Unrestricted Subsidiaries.

**Events of Default:**
Standard for facilities of this nature, including non-payment, breach of covenants (with 30 day cure period for financial covenants), cross-default > $10M.

**Governing Law:** State of New York.
