Effective Communication

How to Communicate Effectively with Different Stakeholders

Effective communication is essential for success as a credit analyst. You need to be able to communicate effectively with a variety of stakeholders, from borrowers to senior management.

Communicating with Borrowers

  • Be clear and concise: Don't use jargon or technical terms that the borrower may not understand.
  • Be respectful: Even if you have to deliver bad news, always be respectful.
  • Be responsive: Respond to the borrower's questions and concerns in a timely manner.

Communicating with Senior Management

  • Be prepared: Before you meet with senior management, make sure you have all the facts. Be prepared to explain the situation clearly and concisely.
  • Be confident: You're the expert on this credit. Be confident in your assessment and be prepared to defend it.
  • Be focused on the "so what?": Senior management is busy. They don't have time for a lot of detail. Focus on the "so what?" of your analysis.
